ABOUT US

Barbeque Paneer Tikka

Barbeque Paneer Tikka is a dish seasoned with spices, mainly associated with rich Asian cuisine. It's made from chunks of paneer marinated in flavorful spices and grilled in a tandoor. As a vegetarian alternative to chicken tikka and other meat dishes, it has become a popular choice in India and among Indian diaspora communities worldwide.
